# About Me:
Hi, I’m Raksha, a full stack developer who enjoys building things that actually do something in the real world.<br><br>I work across the stack using React, Node, TypeScript, FastAPI, and whatever else gets the job done, but I’m especially drawn to stitching together full systems where frontend, backend, data, and ML all come together into something usable end to end. Recently, that has meant building a dermoscopic image classifier that combines vision with patient metadata and explainability, and a real time predictive maintenance system that streams telemetry through Kafka to anticipate failures before they happen. I’m less interested in models in isolation, more in what happens when they meet messy, real world data. <br><br>I like thinking in layers, how data flows, how systems behave under pressure, and how decisions get made, both by machines and by people. That curiosity spills over into my non technical interests too. I enjoy reading literature, and I’m particularly drawn to conversations that sit at the intersection of algorithms, philosophy, and human behavior.<br><br>Outside of code, you’ll probably find me exploring ideas, overthinking architectures, technical and otherwise, or trying to find a balance between building fast and building well.<br><br>Always up for interesting problems and even more interesting conversations.
